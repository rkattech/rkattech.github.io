Gist

Smart Inbox for Busy Parents

Parents drown in school emails, activity updates, and calendar chaos spread across inboxes. Gist consolidates it all, uses AI to extract what actually matters, and delivers a daily action plan organized by child. No more digging — Gist gives you the gist.

  • Unified inbox for all school and activity emails across multiple accounts
  • AI extracts deadlines, registrations, and action items automatically
  • Child-centric daily briefing shows exactly what matters for each kid
  • Integrated calendar with school events, practices, and deadlines
  • Invite co-parents and caregivers to stay in sync
