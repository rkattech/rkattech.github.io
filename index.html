  • connecting messy real-world data

Technical Brief

We build production AI by separating task decomposition from model execution. Raw inputs flow through secure connectors into lightweight parser agents; extracted entities and canonical representations are persisted in a versioned semantic store. An orchestrator composes agent outputs into product responses; MLOps layers provide rollout controls, observability, and automated rollback. We prioritize data lineage, human-in-the-loop corrections, and ephemeral demos to keep production safe and auditable.

Gist

One inbox. Every kid. Nothing missed.

Your kids' schools, sports leagues, and activities all send email to different accounts. Important deadlines get buried under newsletters and reply-alls. Gist pulls everything into one place, figures out what needs action, and organizes it by child — so you see exactly what matters for Emma today, and what can wait.

  • Connects all your email accounts into one feed
  • Spots deadlines, permission slips, and sign-ups buried in long emails
  • Daily briefing organized by child — Emma's day, Jake's day, all separate
  • Syncs school events, practice schedules, and due dates to your calendar
  • Share with your co-parent, nanny, or grandparents so everyone sees the same plan
