About Us

Technology Should Simplify, Not Complicate

At Hoot Ventures, we're focused on building AI-powered applications that streamline everyday life — reducing digital clutter, automating tedious tasks, and helping people make smarter decisions faster.

Our mission is to remove friction from daily life by creating intuitive, human-centered tools powered by artificial intelligence. We bring practical innovation to life, with a focus on clarity, efficiency, and usability.

We combine deterministic, reliable software with cutting-edge AI to create new experiences that weren't possible before — products that feel like magic but are grounded in real engineering.

Gist

Smart Inbox for Busy Parents

Gist is an AI-powered family assistant that turns scattered school emails, activity updates, and calendar events into a clear, child-centric daily action plan. No more digging through your inbox — Gist gives you the gist.

  • Unified inbox for all school and activity emails across multiple accounts
  • AI extracts deadlines, registrations, and action items automatically
  • Child-centric daily briefing shows exactly what matters for each kid
  • Integrated calendar with school events, practices, and deadlines
  • Invite co-parents and caregivers to stay in sync
