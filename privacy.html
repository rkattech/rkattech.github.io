Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Hoot Ventures LLC ("Hoot Ventures," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our applications and services, including the Gist app ("Gist") and our website at www.hootventures.ai.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials through Google OAuth. We do not store your Google password.

Email and Calendar Data

When you connect your Gmail account(s) to Gist, we access and process email messages and Google Calendar events to provide our services. Specifically, we access:

  • Email metadata (sender, recipient, subject, date)
  • Email content (body text) for AI-powered extraction of actionable information
  • Calendar events and details

You may connect up to three (3) Gmail accounts. We only access data necessary to provide the service.

Family Information

Gist processes your emails to identify and organize information about your children, their schools, teachers, and extracurricular activities. This information is derived from your email content and is used solely to provide you with organized, child-centric summaries.

Usage Data

We collect standard usage analytics including pages visited, features used, device type, and general interaction patterns to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve Gist and our other services
  • Extract and organize actionable information from your emails (deadlines, events, tasks)
  • Generate personalized daily briefings for each child
  • Sync calendar events and reminders
  • Enable collaboration features (e.g., caregiver invitations)
  • Send transactional communications (account verification, service updates)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our products

3. AI Processing

Gist uses artificial intelligence to process your email and calendar data. This includes:

  • Natural language processing to extract facts, deadlines, and action items from emails
  • Semantic analysis to associate information with the correct child and context
  • Summarization to create concise daily briefings

AI processing is performed using secure third-party AI services (such as Google's Gemini API). Data sent to these services is transmitted securely and is not used to train their general models. We only share the minimum data necessary for processing.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use third-party services to operate our platform (cloud hosting, AI processing, email delivery). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Caregivers: If you invite a co-parent or caregiver to your Gist account, they will have access to shared family information within the app.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Safety: We may disclose information if we believe it is necessary to protect the safety of any person or to prevent illegal activity.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Secure authentication via OAuth 2.0
  • User-scoped data isolation (your data is never accessible to other users)
  • Regular security reviews and updates

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Gist is designed for use by parents and guardians. The app is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Information about children is provided indirectly through parents' email data and is used solely to organize information for the parent or guardian.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect your Gmail account(s) at any time
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@hootventures.ai.

9. Google API Services User Data Policy

Gist's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We only use Google user data to provide and improve user-facing features of Gist. We do not use Google user data for advertising or transfer it to third parties for unrelated purposes.

10. Third-Party Links

Our services may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
