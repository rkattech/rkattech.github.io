Gist — Smart Inbox for Parents

Gist is an AI-powered family assistant that helps busy parents manage school communications, events, and activities in one organized place. It consolidates emails from schools, teachers, and activity providers, then uses AI to extract deadlines, action items, and calendar events — presenting everything in a clean, child-centric daily briefing.

Available on: iOS and Web (Android coming soon)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Gist?

Gist is a smart inbox designed for parents. It connects to your Gmail account(s) and automatically organizes school-related emails, extracts important deadlines and action items, and presents everything in a daily briefing organized by child. Think of it as your personal assistant for managing your kids' school and activity communications.

How does Gist access my email?

Gist connects to your Gmail through Google's secure OAuth 2.0 authentication. You sign in with your Google account and grant Gist permission to read your emails. We never see or store your Google password. You can disconnect your account at any time from the app settings.

Is my email data safe?

Yes. We take data security seriously. Your email data is encrypted in transit and at rest, scoped to your account only, and processed solely to provide Gist's features. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I connect multiple email accounts?

Yes, you can connect up to three Gmail accounts to Gist. This is helpful if you use separate email accounts for different children's schools or activities.

Can I invite my spouse or co-parent?

Yes. Gist supports caregiver invitations, allowing you to invite a co-parent or guardian to collaborate and stay in sync on your children's schedules and action items.

How does the AI work?

Gist uses a multi-stage AI pipeline to process your emails. It extracts key facts (dates, deadlines, events, requirements), associates them with the correct child, deduplicates information across multiple emails, and synthesizes everything into a clear daily briefing. The AI continuously learns from new emails to keep your information up to date.

What if the AI makes a mistake?

While our AI is designed for accuracy, it may occasionally misinterpret information. We recommend verifying critical deadlines and details from the original email, which is always linked from within the app. If you notice recurring issues, please let us know at support@hootventures.ai so we can improve.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the app settings. This will permanently remove all your data, including connected email accounts, extracted information, and family profiles. If you need assistance, email us at support@hootventures.ai and we will process your request within 30 days.

Is Gist free?

Gist offers a free tier with core features. Premium features may be available through a subscription. Pricing details will be available on the app and our website when applicable.

What platforms is Gist available on?

Gist is available on iOS and as a web application. Android support is planned for a future release.
